About Prakash

A passionate student and technology creator dedicated to teaching technology in Tamil. Prakash Space exists to break complex ideas — AI, code, Linux, productivity — into lessons anyone can follow, no matter where they're starting from.

🎯
Mission
Make technology education accessible in Tamil — free, practical, and built for beginners.
🔭
Vision
A generation of Tamil-speaking creators, developers, and innovators who never had to choose between their language and their ambitions.
💎
Values
Clarity over complexity. Community over competition. Consistency over hype.
Future Goals
  • Launch structured, beginner-friendly Tamil courses in AI & programming
  • Build an active learner community around Prakash Space
  • Publish open-source projects and free learning resources
  • Grow Prakash Space into a full learning platform, not just a channel

Frequently Asked Questions

What does Prakash Space teach?

AI, programming (Python, C++), Linux, productivity, and career guidance — all explained in Tamil.

Who is this for?

Complete beginners, students, and anyone who finds it easier to learn technology in their own language.

Is the content free?

Yes — videos are free to watch. A dedicated resources hub with notes, cheat sheets, and roadmaps is on the way.

What language are lessons in?

Tamil, with technical terms explained clearly so nothing gets lost in translation.
